# Product

## Register

product

## Users

QuestXP is for self-directed learners, students, and early professionals who use YouTube playlists as informal courses but need structure, progress tracking, accountability, and generated study support.

Users are usually in a focused study workflow. They want to create a course from a playlist, resume the next lecture, review notes, answer quizzes, and track progress without feeling like they are inside a game lobby.

## Product Purpose

QuestXP turns YouTube playlists into structured, gamified courses. The product succeeds when a learner can quickly understand what to study next, trust the generated material, and see steady progress through XP, streaks, and completion state.

## Brand Personality

Disciplined, capable, encouraging.

The product should feel like a serious learning workspace with light gamification, not an arcade or esports dashboard.

## Anti-references

Avoid neon gaming dashboards, purple-blue gradients, excessive glow, glassmorphism, generic AI SaaS hero visuals, overdecorated cards, and loud achievement styling that competes with studying.

## Design Principles

1. Study first, rewards second.
2. Make state obvious: processing, ready, failed, locked, complete.
3. Use restraint so AI features feel trustworthy.
4. Keep gamification as feedback, not decoration.
5. Prefer familiar product patterns over invented visual effects.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast. Avoid relying on color alone for status. Support reduced motion. Keep forms and controls familiar, keyboard accessible, and readable on laptop and mobile screens.
